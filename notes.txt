Just a list of things to keep in mind.



WRAPPERS
========

We will want to keep track of how many messages are sent and recieved. Nodes are ignorant of this, 
so have a mapping in the wrapper class that counts this. See OLSRWrapper.java as an example.




OLSR
=====

WILLINGNESS is abstracted away. Random selection for the MPR election implies that the randomly 
selected node had the highest willingness.
